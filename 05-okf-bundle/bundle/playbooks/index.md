## Playbooks

### Playbook

- [Forecast Revision](forecast_revision.md) - Decision rules and process for recommending a demand forecast revision in Logility, triggered when actuals consistently deviate from plan in a single direction.
- [New Product Performance Review](new_product_review.md) - A structured reasoning process for assessing recently introduced product sales performance against forecast and surfacing inventory risk recommendations.
- [Stockout Escalation](stockout_escalation.md) - Decision rules for assessing stockout risk severity and determining the appropriate escalation and remediation action.
- [Supply Plan Review](supply_plan_review.md) - Exception-based reasoning playbook for interpreting the Logility supply plan to identify SI negatives, safety stock gaps, and fulfillment shortfalls, and to prioritize recommended actions.
