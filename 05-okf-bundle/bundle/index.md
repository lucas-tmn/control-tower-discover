# OKF Supply Chain Knowledge Bundle

A living knowledge base documenting datasets, metrics, business definitions, and decision playbooks for supply chain reasoning agents.

**Status**: Active — MVP build in progress. Domain priority: Demand/Supply Planning → Inventory → Procurement → Fulfillment → Supplier Performance → Warehouse Operations.

## Subdirectories

- [playbooks](playbooks/index.md) - Agent playbooks and step-by-step reasoning workflows for supply chain analysis.
- [processes](processes/index.md) - Recurring business processes and decision workflows that orchestrate when major decisions happen.
- [datasets](datasets/index.md) - Data asset references with warehouse table definitions, grain, source system, and key fields, and commonly used queries.
- [metrics](metrics/index.md) - KPI definitions with business meaning, calculation logic, and interpretation.
- [entities](entities/index.md) - Canonical business object definitions for core supply chain concepts.
- [glossary](glossary/index.md) - Organization-specific term definitions that differ from standard usage.
