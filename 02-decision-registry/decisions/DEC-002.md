## Note on relationship to DEC-001

Both DEC-001 and DEC-002 are served by the **same underlying report and semantic
model** (Forecast Accuracy) but represent genuinely different decisions — one
financial (justifying a tool investment), one performance-management (coaching
planners). This is a direct, evidence-based test of Schema v0.2's design principle
2.2 ("a report may serve many decisions") — confirmed true in practice, not just
in theory.
