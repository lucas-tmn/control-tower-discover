# Business Handbook

Domain-organized business narrative, cutting across the AS-IS evidence,
Decision Registry, TO-BE (OKF) definitions, and the Fabric build — for readers
who want to understand a business domain end-to-end without jumping between
report-level analyses.

**Confidence level for every chapter:** Model-only — built from artifacts, not
yet confirmed by the people who do this work. Each chapter states this
explicitly and flags unsourced claims as Track B questions rather than
presenting them as settled fact. This is the one layer in the repo meant to be
read and trusted at face value by non-technical readers (PO, leadership) — so
every claim traces to a specific source file, and gaps are stated as gaps.
