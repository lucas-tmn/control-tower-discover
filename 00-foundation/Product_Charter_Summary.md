# Product Charter Summary

## Eagle Eye
A Decision Intelligence Platform that continuously improves business
decision quality.

## What it is
The ability to turn a supply chain situation into a decision that is
**prioritized, explained, trusted, actioned, and measured.**

## What it is NOT
- A dashboard
- A report
- A chatbot or Copilot
- A data warehouse
- A UI

## How it creates value
Today, a "situation" (demand spike, stockout risk, forecast drift) is
detected somewhere in 26 disconnected reports, interpreted differently by
whoever looks at it, and acted on inconsistently. Eagle Eye's value is
cutting the time and inconsistency between **"something changed"** and
**"the right action was taken"** — by governing the decision knowledge that
sits between the two.

## North Star
Continuously improve business decision quality.
